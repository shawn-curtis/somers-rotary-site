Our Program

Supporting Somers Students

The Rotary Club of Somers Scholarship Program recognizes graduating high school seniors who have demonstrated academic dedication, community involvement, and the spirit of "Service Above Self." Funded largely through our [Annual Fundraiser Name] and the generosity of local supporters, these awards help students pursue college, trade school, and other paths beyond graduation.

Each year we award [Number] scholarships of [Scholarship Award Amount] each. Recipients are selected by a committee of club members based on the application, an essay, and a record of service — not simply grades. We believe character and commitment matter just as much as a transcript.

Scholarship recipients are honored at our Scholarship Awards Night and celebrated in our community news.

The Four-Way Test

Of the things we think, say, or do:

  1. Is it the TRUTH?
  2. Is it FAIR to all concerned?
  3. Will it build GOODWILL and better friendships?
  4. Will it be BENEFICIAL to all concerned?
